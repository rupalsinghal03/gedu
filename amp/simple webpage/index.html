Course Overview

The Higher National Diploma (HND) is a practical and industry-focused course, giving you the specialist business knowledge and the workplace skills that employers around the world are looking for.
This HND International in Business course at GBS Dubai delivers in-depth insight into the world of modern business, and through assignments and projects, you'll put into practice your newly discovered business skills and knowledge.
The two-year HND in Business course allows you to take a deep dive into your subject and allows for specialisation with one of the course's exciting specialist pathways.

The pathway options help you to develop a higher level of specialist skills and knowledge, and become the qualified professional that top employers are looking for.

Pathways

  • Business
  • Accounting and Finance
  • Management
  • Entrepreneurship and Small Business Management
  • Human Resource Management
  • Marketing
  • Procurement and Supply Management
  • Law

Whether you are looking to join an industry-leading company, or planning to start your own business, these business courses introduce you to all you need to start building a successful career at management level.

 

What Will You Achieve?

  1. Widen your understanding of the global business environment.
  2. Develop specialist knowledge and practical skills through research, independent study, directed study, and workplace scenarios.
  3. Gain transferable skills to enhance your employability and career prospects.
  4. Build the skills and knowledge to progress, earning credits towards a bachelor's degree.

 

Assessment

There are no formal examinations for this course. Assessments are undertaken in various ways:

  • presentation
  • writing an assignment
  • analysing a case study
  • completing a portfolio of evidence
  • writing reports

You will be required to work in your own time to complete research, homework tasks or group work. Self-study is vital to your success on this course and lecturers will give you guidance on how best to approach this throughout the study period.

FAQ

The Higher National Diploma (HND) is equivalent to the first two years of an undergraduate degree. While the duration of HND courses can vary on occasion, they typically take a maximum of two years to complete. HNDs are available across a wide range of specialisms including accounting, business computing, engineering and many more.

Yes, you can turn your Higher National Diploma (HND) into a full degree. There are different pathways available for students who want to take their studies further and gain a full undergraduate degree, which would typically require an additional year of study. Many higher education institutions will accept HND courses for entry to the third and final year of an undergraduate degree.

A Higher National Diploma (HND) and a bachelor’s degree are both great options, but it depends on what you’re looking for. The key differences between an HND and a bachelor’s degree are the length of the course, the intensity of study and the level of the award received at the end. The HND typically takes two years while a bachelor's degree can take up to four years. If you want to fast-track your career prospects then the HND might be better, but if you want a more complete higher education experience then a bachelor's degree might be the preferable option.

When comparing HND vs degree, making the right choice will depend on what you want to accomplish and the type of learning experience you want. As a full degree typically takes double the time to attain, the HND is seen as a quicker route to the job market and has career-based course structures. Taking the quickest path isn’t always the way to go, and those who want a more traditional experience should also consider bachelor's degree courses.

A Higher National Certificate (HNC) and a Higher National Diploma (HND) in Business are both vocational qualifications that provide an essential understanding of the modern international business landscape with practical skills development. The HNC qualification takes one year to acquire and the HND takes two years.
